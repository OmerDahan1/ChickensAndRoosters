how to sign up?

Chickens

  • In order to sign up to the program, you need to do several things:
    1. Press the "Chickens" button.
    2. Answer us about some questions.
    3. Submit your answers when you finish.
    4. If we will find you qualified we will set you "Roosters" according to your preference.
  • During the semester you need to fill your hours in the website each time you meet with them.
  • when you finished to fill the file press the button: "Save File".

Roosters

  • In order to sign up to the program, you need to do several things:
    1. Press the "Roosters" button.
    2. Answer us about some questions.
    3. Submit your answers when you finish.
    4. If we will find you qualified we will set you a "Chicken".
